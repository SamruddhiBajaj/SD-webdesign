INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The department endeavors to be recognized globally as a centre of academic excellence & research in Information Technology.
To inculcate research culture among students by imparting information technology related fundamental knowledge, recent technology trends and ethics to get recognized as globally acceptable and socially responsible professionals.

The department of Information Technology is well known for academic excellence, research culture delivered through it’s undergraduate and postgraduate programs. The Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) Programme in Information Technology started in the academic year 2001-2002 with an intake capacity of 60 and currently running with intake capacity of 180 students . The Master of Engineering (M.E.) programme started in academic year 2011-2012 with an intake capacity of 18 students. Department of Information Technology is recognized for imparting core knowledge, advanced technological trends and ethics to it’s student for better employability The Department offers best infrastructure and computing facilities to it’s stakeholders. A team of 40 Teaching and 10 Technical Staff members under the leadership of Dr. A.M.Bagade unanimously harnesses its potential in all aspects to take the department to greater heights.

  • Excellent academic track record with significant rank holders at University level.
  • Strong Industry Institute collaboration through sponsored laboratories and MoUs.
  • Comprehensive Training programs, most desirable certificate programs and motivating Co Curricular activities.
  • Highly qualified and motivated staff members.
  • Highly professional academic projects
